# ATLAS

Master Knowledge System for Lena Faber

---

## Mission

Atlas is the canonical structure behind lenafaber.com.

It unifies stories, photography, journeys, projects, concepts, publications, places, and timeline into a single connected system.

Atlas is not a blog.

Atlas is not a portfolio.

Atlas is not a gallery.

Atlas is a knowledge network.

Every piece of content exists once and can be discovered through multiple paths.

---

# Core Principles

## Single Source of Truth

Every item exists only once.

No duplicated stories.

No duplicated photographs.

No duplicated projects.

Relationships create multiple views.

---

## Origin and Meaning Are Independent

A photograph may originate from:

* Route 66

while simultaneously belonging to:

* Architecture
* Geometry
* Industrial
* Arizona
* 2015
* Brave

Origin and interpretation are separate.

---

## Everything Is Connected

Every entity may connect to any other entity.

Stories connect to journeys.

Journeys connect to places.

Places connect to photographs.

Photographs connect to projects.

Projects connect to concepts.

Concepts connect to stories.

The system is network-based, not hierarchy-based.
